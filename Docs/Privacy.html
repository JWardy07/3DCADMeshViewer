Privacy Policy for 3D CAD & Mesh Viewer

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

3D CAD & Mesh Viewer is designed to view 3D model files on your device. We do not require account registration.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through the app.

Files You Import

When you import files, they are processed on your device and stored in the app’s local sandbox so you can view them later.

We do not upload your model files to our servers.

Cloud Storage and Files App

If you import from iCloud Drive, Google Drive, Dropbox, or other providers via Apple Files, those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Analytics, Tracking, and Ads

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can delete imported files at any time from within the app.

You can also remove the app to delete local app data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
